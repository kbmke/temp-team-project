Our story

Palomino Bar is an old school spot in the great neighborhood of Bay View - Milwaukee, WI.

We love good food and good drink. The majority of our ingredients are sourced locally and our Southern inspired, belly-warming food is made all from scratch.

We've got one of the best whiskey lists in the city (and Wisconsin, actually), great handcrafted cocktails, well chosen craft beers as well as ice cold, crispy brews from the Big Boys. And the pie is great too.
